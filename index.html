Bluff Den

In the den, only the clever survive.

Game Setup

Game Rules

1. Game Overview

In Bluff Den, players are divided into two secret teams each round: the Foxes, who know a secret word, and a single Rabbit, who is left in the dark. The goal is to be the first to 25 🥕 carrots.

  • Players: 4+
  • Roles: 1 Rabbit, rest Foxes
  • Starting Carrots: 10 🥕 per player
  • Bet Range: 1–3 🥕 carrots

2. The Flow of a Round

  1. The Word Grid Reveal (Pass the Phone): The app privately shows each player the 3x6 Word Grid. Foxes see the Secret Word highlighted; the Rabbit does not.
  2. Broad Clue Round (Verbal): Each player gives a single-word or short-phrase clue out loud.
  3. The Wager: All players secretly bet 🥕 carrots. Foxes bet on catching the Rabbit. The Rabbit bets on guessing the word.
  4. Second Clue Round (Verbal): Each player gives a second, more specific clue out loud.
  5. The Verdict: All players vote on who the Rabbit is. The Rabbit secretly guesses the word from the grid.

3. Scoring: The Interdependent Payout

Payouts depend on two things: Did the Foxes catch the Rabbit? (Catch Success) And did the Rabbit guess the word? (Guess Success). A 2x Sabotage Bonus is applied if your team succeeds AND the other team fails.

  • Scenario 1: Decisive Fox Victory (Catch Success, Guess Failure)
    Foxes win their bets x2. Rabbit loses their bet.
  • Scenario 2: Masterful Rabbit Escape (Catch Failure, Guess Success)
    Rabbit wins their bet x3. Foxes lose their bets.
  • Scenario 3: A Contested Outcome (Catch Success, Guess Success)
    Bonuses are cancelled. Everyone gets their bets back.
  • Scenario 4: Total Failure (Catch Failure, Guess Failure)
    Nobody wins. All bets are lost and removed from the game.

4. End of the Game

The game ends if a player reaches 25 🥕 carrots, a player's score hits 0, or after 10 rounds. The player with the highest score wins.
